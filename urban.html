Chairman's Message

At Thodupuzha Urban Co-Operative Bank Ltd.; there are some core values we bring to work every day. The co-operative banking has been playing distinct and significant role in the process of socio-economic development of the region and in the whole country.

As far as the state of Kerala is concerned, Bank serves as a balancing centre for the surplus resources of co-operatives in the state and there by ensures their multifaceted development and progress. It also provides normal banking services to its customers by introducing and adopting latest technologies like RTGS, IMPS, NEFT etc. Besides, granting loans, the bank serves as an outlet for investment. Further the bank have planned the implemented core banking facility.

We thank all our customers (Depositors, Share Holders and Borrowers) for their valuable support and profound confidence shown by them in us. I wish to assure you that we are committed to offer delightful banking experience to all our regular or even casual clients. We try to compete with our past performance and constantly try to upgrade our products and services, systems and procedures. We must heartily welcome any suggestions coming our way from our customers and any member of the public. We are thankful to one and all who are connected directly or indirectly with us.

V V Mathai (Chairman)
